An exploration of multistable perception in Virtual Reality
Dar Cria is a VR experience born from a desire to explore the potential of bringing Vier Nevâ€™s body of work into Virtual Reality.

While exploring an experimental narrative about birth and art making, Dar Cria challenges the user's perception of the surrounding environment through the use of imagery with multiple meanings. Interactive portals connect the many spaces that form the experience, as well as help the users identify another possible interpretation of what they see.

The project was created and developed by Vier Nev with Sound Design by Francisca Dores.
