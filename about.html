Vier Nev is a Portuguese multidisciplinary artist.

Vier is interested in creating experiences that play with perception and explore the artistic potential of emerging technologies. 

_

Vier studied Multimedia at Escola Superior de Artes e Design (ESMAD) and Virtual Reality at London College of Communication, University of the Arts London.  They have worked in AR and VR development, animation and art direction.

Vier's latest projects include the award-winning short film "A Mind Sang", AR exhibition "A Terra é Tela" as well as the VR experiences "Dar Cria" and "Lúcido".
